English:
As summer solstice nears, three cities sway,
Madrid basks in 38-degree heat of day,
Vienna's 34 degrees, a gentle breeze,
Aalborg's 22, with winds that bring the seas.
Madrid's UV index high, a caution to take,
Vienna's and Aalborg's, a safer shade to make.

Spanish:
Mañana, ¿dónde estaría bien?
No en Madrid, con calor que abrasa el suelo,
Ni en Aalborg, con vientos que cortan como cuchillo,
Sino en Viena, con 34 grados y un cielo sereno,
Donde el sol brilla con suavidad, y la brisa es un susurro.
Tomorrow, where would it be nice to be?
Not in Madrid, with scorching heat on the ground,
Nor in Aalborg, with cutting winds like a knife,
But in Vienna, with 34 degrees and a serene sky,
Where the sun shines gently, and the breeze is a whisper.
