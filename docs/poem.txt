As tomorrow dawns, Madrid's warmth abides,
With 25 degrees, a gentle, sunny stride.
No rain falls, and the breeze is light,
A pleasant day, with UV index in sight.
Vienna's cooler, with a stronger gust,
Aalborg's chill, with daylight that we trust.
But if I had to choose, I'd say Madrid's the place,
To bask in warmth and sunshine's gentle pace.

Mañana, Madrid brilla con suave calor,
mientras Viena y Aalborg enfrentan un clima más frio y ventoso.
Con 25 grados y un índice UV de 7,4, Madrid es el destino ideal,
donde el sol brilla y el viento es suave, un día perfecto para disfrutar.
